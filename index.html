About me

Hi. My name is Nikola Kotur, but people usually call me Kotnik. I am Serbian living in France and working for the Commerce Guys. There I help build awesome products, play with the latest technology and make a part of the team that brings Drupal platform to a whole new level.

My programming development started early, and the path is typical for the 80's generation: learning Basic the first, then jumping into assembly and C, while having passionate affaire with Pascal at the same time. I used FORTRAN for a while as well, during my obsession with math in the early high school.

Now I work with Python and PHP exclusively, but I am actively looking for an excuse to use Erlang.

Aside from programming, I like to play chess, read books about zombies and watch movies in that genre. I am blogging too.

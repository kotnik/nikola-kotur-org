About me

I am a Serbian living in Paris and working for Commerce Guys where I build and create awesome new products, play with the latest technologies and play my part in bringing Drupal platform to a whole new level.

I started programming rather early, and my path is somewhat typical for the 80s generation: learning Basic, then jumping into assembly and C, after a passionate affaire with Pascal. I programmed FORTRAN for a while as well, during my obsession with mathematics in the early high school. Now I work with Python and PHP, but actively look for an excuse to use Erlang.

I learned PHP and within weeks I was writing my own CMS, as we all did - I am sure. Researching for it, I was paying more and more attention to the Drupal CMS. Not long after, I was using Drupal wherever I could, and using it professionally.

Aside from programming, I like to play chess, read books about zombies, as well as watching movies in that genre. I am preparing to blog again, too.
